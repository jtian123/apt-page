Search for the perfect home

Browse Apateu’s listings by location and move-in/move-out dates. Instead of generic tags, every listing displays the host’s own description, so you get all the crucial details in their own words.

Like, Bid & Match

Spotted a listing you love? Tap the “Like” button to send your interest. Hosts will review your profile and bid before deciding whether to match with you.

Chat & Negotiate

we have built-in chat functionality, so you can communicate directly with the host and other potential roommates. Ask questions, share your preferences, and get to know each other better before making a decision.

Safety & Trust

Apateu prioritizes your safety and trust. We verify user ID to ensure safety and authenticity. For students, we also verify their student status by .edu email. In addition, we make report button available in every chat and every post, so that you can report any suspicious activity or content.
